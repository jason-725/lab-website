Our Collaborations

US Space Force: We are working on multiple classified projects for the United States Space Force.

Gavii.ai: Gavii.ai, an Aggie-owned company, has hired lab members to collaborate in the area of AI and Cybersecurity.

USAF 16th Air Force: We have a MoU with the 16th AF Cyber, HQ at Lackland AFB. Our lab members are working on three projects for the 16th Air Force Cyber.

IIT Roorkee: Texas A&M and IIT Roorkee have an MOA, and our lab is working on multiple research projects and exchange programs with IIT Roorkee.
