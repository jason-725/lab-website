About the Lab

Our Mission: AGGIES lab, or AI for Government, Grid, Infrastructure, & Engineering Security, exists to promote cybersecurity and safety in a world of rapidly evolving AI technologies. Through state-of-the-art research and industry-leading projects, AGGIES focuses on developing solutions that can transform digital security for applications within fields such as computation and data sciences, social sciences, medical applications, and the humanities. At AGGIES, global challenges are met with technical and academic prowess to provide novel, domain-specific, results.
