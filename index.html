Our Vision

To establish Texas A&M and the Great State of Texas as national leaders in AI-driven security for government, grid, and infrastructure systems, while inspiring our Lab members to shape the future of national resilience and global innovation.

Our Mission

At the AI for Government, Grid, Infrastructure & Engineering Security (AGGIES Lab), our mission is to help establish Texas A&M University and the Great State of Texas as national leaders in AI-driven security across government, critical infrastructure, and engineered systems.

We advance national resilience and global innovation by developing pioneering AI solutions at the intersection of cybersecurity, operational technology, and information technology. Through interdisciplinary research, real-world collaboration, and a commitment to discovery and leadership, we empower AGGIES Lab members to drive transformational change, protect vital systems, and serve the public good.

Our Mission

To promote cybersecurity and safety in a rapidly evolving world through state-of-the-art research and industry leading projects.
