Featured Research

Empowering a Safer Tomorrow

AGGIES Lab, or AI for Government, Grid, Infrastructure, & Engineering Security, exists to promote cybersecurity and safety in a world of rapidly evolving AI technologies. Through state of the art research and industry leading projects, we focus on developing solutions that can transform digital security within fields such as computation and data sciences, social sciences, medical applications, and the humanities. At AGGIES, global challenges are met with technical and academic prowess to provide novel, high-impact results.


Our Mission

Our mission is to help establish Texas A&M University and the Great State of Texas as national leaders in AI-driven security across government, critical infrastructure, and engineered systems.

We strengthen national resilience and global innovation by developing pioneering AI solutions at the intersection of cybersecurity, operational technology, and information technology. Through interdisciplinary research, real-world collaboration, and a commitment to discovery and leadership, we empower AGGIES Lab members to drive transformational change, protect vital systems, and serve the public good.
