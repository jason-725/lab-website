Success Stories, Events and Visits

Houston OTC: Our research on maritime cybersecurity, "Cybersecurity bridges the gap" got selected for the University R&D Showcase at Offshore Technology Conference (OTC) at Houston.

Industry Visit: The AGGIES Lab welcomed Dr. Rajagopalan, VP of Operations at NVIDIA, who shared valuable career advice and personal anecdotes from the rise of the tech industry.

USSF Visit: Our lab hosted representatives from the United States Space Force to discuss ongoing projects and explore potential collaborations in cybersecurity and artificial intelligence.

Lackland AFB Visit: Several members toured Lackland Air Force Base, command headquarters of the 16th Air Force. We engaged with defense personnel and explored opportunities for future projects in cybersecurity and AI applications.

Student Research Week: Our team proudly showcased our research during SRW, fostering meaningful connections within the academic community and winning several award categories.

CSCE Poster Expo: Our lab presented current and completed research and projects, engaging with students and faculty to discuss innovations in cybersecurity and AI.

Guest Speaker: Hosted Chris Scotti, Director of Strategic Partnerships at Texas A&M, to discuss emerging trends in the digital security space.

Diwali Celebration: Our lab came together to celebrate Diwali, honoring culture and community with traditional food, music, and dance.

Grad Students Appreciation: Celebrating one of many published papers in Spring 2025. We honored our graduate researchers' dedication and collaborative spirit, highlighting innovative work.

ICCWS Williamsburg 2025, Virginia: Presenting our research with AGGIES lab affiliate Nakul Singh about AI-Driven Cybersecurity Strategies for ISPs: Balancing Threat Mitigation and Monetization.
