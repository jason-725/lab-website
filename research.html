Research Highlights

Our lab tackles a variety of projects ranging from fundamental science to applied technology. Here are a few of our featured works:

Project Atlas: Mapping Neural Interfaces

An interdisciplinary effort to design and test high-fidelity brain-machine interface systems for medical applications.

This project investigates long-term biocompatibility, signal fidelity, and software decoding mechanisms in real-time control scenarios. Our team collaborates with neurologists and bioengineers.

Smart Materials for Environmental Sensing

Developing polymer-based systems that change properties in response to pollutants and stressors.

These sensors aim to provide affordable, distributed environmental monitoring in areas with limited access to conventional equipment. In collaboration with chemical engineering and sustainability teams.

AI-Augmented Diagnostics

Integrating deep learning algorithms into clinical workflows to support early disease detection.

This research explores model interpretability, ethical data sourcing, and system validation in medical settings. Joint effort with local hospitals and health informatics programs.
